  • FAQ

    Wow, isn't "AntiConsultant" a diss to consultants?

    There are two popular meanings to the prefix anti-. The first is against, like mortal enemies. Antibacterial, antiabortion, antiseptic. The second is dialectical opposite. Antimatter, antihero, antithesis.

    I've spent enough time on teams that have had consultants forced on them from above in an attempt to legitimize the efforts of the team. The consultant usually brings in a set of guidelines and experiences that are not organic to the team and the culture of the company. Of course, there is precious little time to mesh the two cultures. The clash

    What is this website anyway?

    Good question. It's meant to be a portfolio of sorts, but suggestive rather than comprehensive. It's meant to be a brief introduction to my experiences and the concepts I've developed. It's meant to be fun.

    What is the tie-in with astronomy?

    Playing with the parallax effect I thought that it would be fun to see the effect support things orbiting other things.

    Why are elements on the page responding to my mouse or the tilt of my device?

    The real answer is that I find static, predetermined layouts to be boring. Sure there is something fun about scaling a browser window to see how the page responds, but I wanted the user to be able to rearrange the elements more easily.
