The AntiConsultant Approach

Research
Define
  • Balance efficiency & effectiveness
  • Limit hypotheticals
  • Focus on 1-3 issues
  • Avoid entangling assumptions
  • See opportunities during research
Design
Prepare
  • Focus on primary challenges
  • Keep it lightweight
  • Low resolution for standard stuff
  • Remember: design continues to evolve into production phase
  • Consider mobile, tablet, desktop early (but keep loose)
  • ID hot button issues and manage them (with client or stakeholders)
Prototype
Make, Test & Revise
  • Is this a prototype or an early production version?
  • Test early
  • Use short prototyping cycles
  • Don't lose sight of the bigger picture
  • Manage communication with stakeholders
  • Focus on testable features
  • Low resolution buildout
  • Make priority, testable features first
  • Have a clear, general plan for using outcomes
  • Revision plan
  • Discuss
  • Rough out development support documents
Production
Organize
  • Maintain a close inventory of deliverables
  • Create easy development support documentation, including styleguides and diagrams
  • Create a sustainability plan
    • Maintenance
    • Revisions
    • Feeding
    • Emergency
  • Plan for user support
  • Schedule well, including reviews
  • Review QA approach for pre-production and production
  • A/B test planning

C. Consider the Path

  • Minimize release diversity where possible
  • Narrow focus

Agile?
  • Who is closest to owning knowledge to help avoid gaps?
  • What lightweight documentation can help support agile development?
  • How to include efficient research steps?
Lean?
  • Coordinate with teams able to provide analysis data
  • Create meaningful design standards that may weather lean pivots
  • Create navigation and visual elements that will flex easily
