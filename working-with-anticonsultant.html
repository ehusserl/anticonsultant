AntiConsultant provides you with solutions for developing strong, sound experiences, including web sites and applications for large and small online businesses, academic organizations, and individuals. Contact me for a no-charge consultation.

What does AntiConsultant do?

  • Logical communications thinking

    I help you translate your communications ideas into goals and the goals into plans before you engage a team, or determine to build or buy a solution.

    Persona example

  • A sustained, productive relationship

    From consultation through the product's lifecycle, I help you think sustainably about your websites, applications, multimedia authoring, and social networking projects. I analyze website interfaces for large or small scale sites, for example Exploratorium.

    Exploratorium case study

  • Knows Ivy League institutions

    I have over six years experience with higher ed institutions like Cornell University. I have worked at the highest level with university presidents and provosts, as well as with departments, technical staff and individual researchers. I can provide budgets for grants on short notice.

    Cornell University case studyCornell Web plan doc

  • Evolves early products

    I have worked with early products like GetThere.com, the world's first online travel reservations system. We enabled United Airlines to move their business online. I helped the company to specialize their products for both airlines and corporate travel. Seatmaps and flight tracking? You saw them there first.

    Paymap design doc
    Info Arch diagrams

  • Creates concepts for payment systems

    I have worked with international payments leaders like Alibaba.com, Paympap Mortgage Systems and Western Union Small Business.

    Payments case study Alibaba.com case study Payment Control concept

  • Adapts small businesses

    I have helped small businesses and startups go from concept to reality, like TheGardener.com and Gimme! Coffee.

    Gimme! Coffee case study

  • Builds high performance design teams

    I have assembled teams for large scale organizations like Western Union, Cornell University or startups like Internet Travel Network and Paymap. I can help you with UX team recruitment and UX resume review, too.

    Design Leadership overview

  • Assesses usability & recommends

    I help you tailor solutions to the circumstances of your organization. I developed a rich understanding of usability from collaborative study with Cornell University pioneers in the field: CU Ergo and the Interaction Design Lab. I can lead you through low budget, highly focused usability studies for startups and large scale efforts.

  • Optimizes for search engines
